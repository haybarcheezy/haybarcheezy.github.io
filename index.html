Houston Technical SEO

Allow search engines to access, read, interpret & index your site.

Websites can be perfectly up-to-date on the front-end, but taking a look “under the hood” can arise a cause for concern. Can search engine bots index your page? These are all technical SEO questions we ask to ensure a website can be simple for Google to understand and in-turn put your business on the map.

Conversion Rate Optimization

We analyze your analytics and tailor a unique strategy to generate more leads to your business.

Conversion rate optimization (CRO) is the effort to increase the amount of traffic (website visitors) who take a designed action. This could mean increasing the mount of leads that view your website and book an appointment online or the number of direct sales your online store brings in.

Through an analysis of a website, we can strategize best practices to either increase overall traffic to the website or if the site is already generating heavy traffic we can focus on increasing the amount of visitors that convert. The key to a successful CRO strategy is collecting solid web analytics to reinforce your efforts on where to focus.
